About Us

We are two university students currently attending Wilfrid Laurier University in the Waterloo region.
Student life can be difficult, especially when you're unfamiliar with your campus or the new city you're in. That's why we decided to create DiscoverLoo, a website that's meant to help students make the most out of their university experiences through providing detailed and up to date info on the latest events, promotions, and changes in the surrounding area.
We hope you enjoy our site, and feel free to contact us with any questions, comments, or suggestions.
Happy exploring!
