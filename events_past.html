Laurier Boxing Club Free Week

Date: Wed Sep 20

Time: 9 PM - 10:30 PM

Location: Multipurpose Room, Wilfrid Laurier University Stadium

Laurier Boxing Clubpage is offering free boxing lessons to all Wilfrid Laurier University students on Sep. 20th. Students will need to bring their OneCards, gym clothes and running shoes, water, and hand wraps (or $10 for a new pair). Provided are boxing gloves, punching bags, and a coach to teach the class. If you are interested in registering for the entire term, the fee is $60 and covers training on Mondays and Wednesdays and sparring on Fridays. To learn more, visit the Facebook Event Page to learn more.
