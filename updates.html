Region of Waterloo Rapid Transit System (LRT)

Expected Date of Completion: 2017

Expected Date of Opening: 2018

Location: University of Waterloo LRT Stop

The Region of Waterloo rapid transit system will consist of a light rail transit (LRT) going from Conestoga Mall in the City of Waterloo to Fairview Park Mall in the City of Kitchener, a distance of 19 km. There will be stops at University of Waterloo and Laurier-Waterloo Park, and according to the project schedule, the LRT will be completed by 2017 and will begin operation stage 1 in 2018.
